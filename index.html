The Visualization Shows

The visualization shows various stats about some common smartphone brands. More details are available on hover, and data can be sorted based on various attributes.

The Visualization is For:

This visualization is intended for European students seeking to buy a new smartphone - and want to keep it for longer, or worry about a specific component breaking.

The Visualization is important because:

Reducing electronic waste: Understanding common smartphone issues can lead to better design and longer-lasting devices, reducing the need for frequent replacements.

Financial savings: Repairing rather than replacing smartphones can save students money.

Environmental impact: Proper disposal and repair practices contribute to sustainability.

How to Read the Visualization:

Each row shows arious stats about its respective brand's phone's durability. Hover over the headers to get more details on each column. Click the headers to sort the data.
